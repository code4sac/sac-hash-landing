Sacramento Hashtag Launch Party

Live Music - Community Leaders - Drinks - Interactive Twitter Map

The Sacramento Hashtag Project is about to launch an interactive hashtag map to help Sacramentans get #Local and connect with the neighborhoods where they live, work, and play everyday.

Come on out, share a pint with us, and celebrate as the hashtag map (designed by Code4Sacramento) goes live! Find your neighborhood on the map, see your local tweets in live time, and find out what's happening around town.... we'll also teach you how to use twitter.
